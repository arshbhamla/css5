Coraline

Appreciate what you have -- More fun doesn't mean better

Movie Description

Coraline is a story of a young girl who found her old life boring. She struggled to make any meaningful connection with either of her parents. She discovered a doorway to an alternate world, one where her parents loved her unconditionally, but her perception wasn't good per say. The story develops to her coming to a conclusion that this life isn't meant for her.

Characters

  • Coraline Jones: Main Character
  • Charlie Jones: Father
  • Mel Jones: Mother
  • Black Cat: Originally stray cat, comes with Coraline in her adventures
  • Wybie Lovat: Coraline's friend and later boyfriend
